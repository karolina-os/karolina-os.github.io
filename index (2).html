[Whiskers of the North] — final year project, made in unity 2024

[For my final project, I created a small, light-hearted game set in a calm and friendly environment, where the player could explore, interact with animals and collect cats. I used colliders and simple pick-up scripts to build the core interactions, and added dialogue to give the game more personality and charm. As a big cat lover, I often find that the things I create somehow end up being connected to cats, and this project was a fun way to bring that into my game development work.]

about.

I currently work in web content and QA, looking after a large e-commerce websites. My role involves testing pages, finding bugs and broken links, migrating content, building new pages, and keeping everything accurate, consistent and easy to use.

i like the details that make a site both work properly and feel good to use. nothing broken, nothing confusing, everything where you'd expect it.

i have always been interested in how games work and how they are made, which led me to study Games Development. During my degree, I explored different areas of design and development, including writing GDDs, planning levels, developing stories, building prototypes and creating environments in Unity. I also learned the basics of 3D modelling in Autodesk Maya, working on everything from simple objects to furniture and character-style modelling. Not every idea or project was perfect, but each one became a valuable learning experience and helped me better understand the process of creating games..

lately, I have become increasingly interested in UX/UI design and started learning Figma.
